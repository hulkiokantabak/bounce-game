# BOUNCE

A one-thumb browser game. The ball drops. You tap. It bounces. The trail stays.

## [>>> PLAY NOW <<<](https://hulkiokantabak.github.io/bounce-game/)

No download needed — runs in your browser on desktop and mobile.

## How to Play

- **Tap / Click** to place surfaces
- The ball bounces off your surfaces
- Survive rounds as gravity and chaos increase
- Watch the trail your ball leaves behind

## Features

- Ball physics with increasing difficulty
- Surface placement mechanics
- Configurable settings (sound, haptics)
- AI demo mode — watch the bot play
- Developer API via `window.BOUNCE_AI`
- Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
